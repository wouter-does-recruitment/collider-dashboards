Reading the funnel: Of 214 profiles identified and contacted, 73% accepted the connection and 81% of those replied to the message — strong engagement rates. The drop-off happens at application: only 15 applied via the form (7%), but 19 reached interview stage (9%) — meaning some were identified and invited directly without going through the public form. Ultimately 5 candidates from proactive outreach are shortlisted, representing a 2.3% lead-to-shortlist conversion. This is a healthy rate for a niche deep-tech CEO search.
