Key insight: Inbound candidates convert to shortlist at 31% vs 26% for Dripify-sourced candidates. This is expected — inbound applicants self-select and tend to be more motivated/aligned with the programme. However, Dripify adds significant volume and reaches profiles that would never have applied organically. 42% of all interview-stage candidates came from proactive outreach — a meaningful contribution to a pool that would otherwise be much smaller.
