How it works in practice
01
Candidate enters pipeline
Assign one Folder (sector) and all relevant Tags. Takes 2 minutes per candidate in Manatal.
02
New project launches (Cohort 2)
Filter by Folder + Tags. E.g. "Biotech + Tech Transfer + Spain eligible + Disruptive Talent verified" → instant shortlist.
03
Pool grows over time
Every candidate who applied, replied to outreach, or was screened adds to the pool. Compound value across cohorts.
